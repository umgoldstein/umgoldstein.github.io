About me

A year ago i actually wrote my first line of code. The code was written in Matlab for a project in social, cognitive and affective neuroscience.


Before my studies in neuroscience, I chose to major in experimental psychology. I made this decision because i loved studying about human decision-making and problem-solving in the mathematical part of experiments. Soon into my neuroscience studies, i realized that i loved programming and problem solving more than the actual bulk of psychology/neuroscience. Therefore i chose a career in Software Engineering.


Psychology and Web development work hand in hand to me. For programming a site you always have to keep in mind the user interaction. Furthermore your code also has to be engineer friendly, you are not the only one reading your code. Plus both fields have alot of problems solving associated with them.
