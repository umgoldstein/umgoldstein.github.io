About me

I am a software engineer with a background in Ruby, Javascript, Rails, React and much more! Plus I can write my variable names in German and English, which might be helpful someday! My whole life I moved around, which helped me to adapt to new evironments quickly and learn to speak English fluently. First time that I was interested in coding was probably the first time I saw someone program a game. Gaming was always a big passion of mine.

A year ago I wrote my first line of code. The code was written in Matlab for a project in social, cognitive and affective neuroscience. Before my studies in neuroscience, I chose to major in experimental psychology. I made this decision because I enjoy studying human decision-making and problem-solving in the mathematical part of experiments. Soon into my neuroscience studies, I realized that I loved programming and problem solving more than working primarly in the field of psychology/neuroscience. Therefore I chose a career in Software Engineering.

Psychology and Web development work hand in hand to me. For programming a side you always have to keep in mind is the user interaction. Furthermore your code also has to be engineer friendly, you are not the only one reading your code. Plus both fields have a lot of problem solving associated with them.

Long story short: I finally don't just like what I do, I actually love what I do. Some days I will sit there for hours just programming and forgetting time. Back end or Front end code it doesn't matter. Further down you can find some of my projects. Enjoy!
